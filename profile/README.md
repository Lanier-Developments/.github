# Lanier Developments

**AI-augmented engineering, multi-agent systems, and platform modernization.**

Lanier Developments LLC is a principal-engineer-led consulting firm. We help teams
adopt AI into the way they actually build software — not as a demo, but as
durable, governed, production-grade practice. That means multi-agent workflows that
hold up under review, platform and developer-experience work that scales, and the
operational discipline to keep it all auditable.

### What we work on

- **AI-augmented engineering** — embedding AI into real development workflows, with
  the review pipelines and guardrails to make the output trustworthy.
- **Multi-agent orchestration** — designing systems where multiple models and tools
  collaborate, with a human as the synthesis and integration layer.
- **Platform & developer experience** — CI/CD, container supply-chain security, and
  self-hosted infrastructure that teams can actually maintain.
- **Tooling** — CLIs and services that make the above repeatable.
